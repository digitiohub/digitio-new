# Trajectories

Trajectories is a specialized, multi-tenant travel management platform designed to unify the entire workflow for tour operators, DMCs, and luxury travel agencies. Built with a modern tech stack, it bridges the gap between lead generation and final payment, providing a premium experience for both operators and their clients.

## Overview

Trajectories is more than just a customer database. It is a complete operating system for travel businesses, handling everything from the first "Hello" on WhatsApp to the final "Payment Received" notification. It is designed to scale with your agency, supporting multiple brands and complex team structures.

## Problem

Travel operators traditionally struggle with fragmented tools and manual processes:
*   **Scattered Communication:** Leads are often lost across WhatsApp, Email, and Instagram DMs with no central system for follow-ups.
*   **Manual Admin Overload:** Operators spend 4+ hours per client creating manual PDF quotations, copying pricing, and calculating margins.
*   **Disconnected Payments:** Chasing bank transfers and manually reconciling accounts causes delays and operational friction.
*   **Operational Blindspots:** Without a unified dashboard, there is zero visibility into pipeline health, team performance, or actual business growth.

## Solution

Trajectories provides a **single, unified system** for the complete travel workflow. It brings every tool your travel business needs—CRM, itinerary builder, quotation system, and payment gateway—into one powerful, beautifully designed platform. It eliminates tool-switching and professionalizes every client interaction.

## How It Works

1.  **Capture Inquiry:** Leads from any channel are automatically routed into a central inbox and assigned to the right team member.
2.  **Build Itinerary:** Draft visual, day-by-day journeys using a drag-and-drop builder with reusable components.
3.  **Generate Quotation:** Create professional branded PDFs or interactive web quotation links instantly.
4.  **Collect Payment:** Clients review their journey on a premium portal and pay deposits or full amounts via integrated Razorpay.
5.  **Confirm Booking:** The system auto-updates statuses, generates invoices, and manages all post-booking trip documents.

## Features

*   **Customer & Lead Management:** Unified inbox, lead scoring, and full conversation history.
*   **Visual Itinerary Builder:** Efficiently build multi-day trips with saved flights, hotels, and activities.
*   **Smart Quotation System:** Interactive web portals and branded PDFs with automated pricing and margin calculators.
*   **Razorpay Integration:** Secure, frictionless payment flows for instant deposits and automated reconciliation.
*   **Team & Permissions:** Granular Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) for managing large teams and multiple brands.
*   **White-Label Ready:** Fully customizable with your agency’s logo, colors, and custom domain.

## Use Cases

*   **Tour Operators:** For those looking to automate high-volume bookings and scale operations.
*   **Destination Management Companies (DMCs):** To manage complex ground operations and B2B agent relationships.
*   **Luxury Travel Agencies:** To deliver a "high-touch" premium digital experience to discerning travelers.
*   **Independent Planners:** To professionalize their brand appearance and automate administrative tasks.

*   **Unified Workflow:** Keeps your entire business in one tab—no more jumping between spreadsheets, WhatsApp, and banking apps.

Trajectories transforms travel operations from a manual, fragmented process into a streamlined, digital-first powerhouse. It empowers travel professionals to save 15+ hours per week, boost conversion rates by 40%, and deliver a world-class booking experience.
