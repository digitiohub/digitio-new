# Soryouth Renewable Energy - Solar EPC CRM

### Overview
**Soryouth Renewable Energy CRM** is a comprehensive management platform tailored specifically for Solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) companies. It streamlines the entire solar project lifecycle—from initial lead generation and site surveys to installation, commissioning, and long-term maintenance (AMC).

### Problem
Solar EPC companies often face fragmented workflows, relying on disconnected tools for lead tracking, site documentation, inventory management, and customer service. This lack of integration leads to:
- **Delayed Timelines**: Communication gaps between sales, engineering, and ground teams.
- **Inventory Mismatch**: Inaccurate tracking of panels and components across multiple sites.
- **Subsidy Bureaucracy**: Difficulty in managing complex government financing and documentation.
- **Maintenance Gaps**: Poor tracking of post-installation maintenance (AMC) schedules.
- **Data Silos**: Lack of centralized analytics for business performance.

### Solution
Our CRM provides a **single source of truth** for Solar EPC operations. By consolidating all business functions into a unified dashboard, we enable teams to:
- **Automate Workflows**: Transition seamlessly from lead to proposal.
- **Digitize Documentation**: Record site surveys with real-time data entry.
- **Optimize Inventory**: Sync stock levels with active installation requirements.
- **Standardize Service**: Manage service tickets and AMC contracts efficiently.
- **Data-Driven Decisions**: Access real-time KPIs for revenue and performance.

### How It Works
1. **Lead to Proposal**: Capture inquiries and generate technical solar proposals based on customer-specific requirements.
2. **Survey & Design**: Engineers conduct site assessments using mobile-responsive forms, capturing critical technical data and documentation.
3. **Project Execution**: Monitor the project through its pipeline, assigning tasks to specialized teams and tracking milestones.
4. **Operations & Maintenance**: Transition commissioned projects to the AMC phase with automated service ticketing and performance alerts.
5. **Back-Office Integration**: Manage invoicing, subsidies, vendor relationships, and expenses in one place.

### Features
- **Project Pipeline**: Visual tracking for residential, commercial, and industrial installations.
- **Site Survey Module**: Digital technical assessments with document uploads.
- **Inventory Management**: Real-time tracking of modules, inverters, and BOS components.
- **Service & AMC Ticketing**: Portal for maintenance requests and contract renewals.
- **Government Subsidies**: Documentation management for various financing schemes.
- **Performance Analytics**: Dashboards for revenue trends and installation metrics.
- **Communication Hub**: Centralized history of all customer interactions.

### Use Cases
- **Sales Teams**: Quickly convert inquiries into technical proposals and monitor conversion rates.
- **Site Engineers**: Perform assessments in the field with a tablet-optimized interface.
- **Project Owners**: Monitor asset health and ROI through a dedicated customer view.
- **Operations Managers**: Optimize procurement based on real-time project demand and stock levels.

### Why It’s Better
- **Industry-Specific**: Built specifically for the solar EPC workflow, not adapted from generic platforms.
- **Mobile-First**: Fully responsive UI designed for field work and office management alike.
- **Unified Ecosystem**: Seamlessly integrates technical data with financial and inventory modules.
- **Premium User Experience**: Built with a high-performance, dark-mode supported UI using React, TypeScript, and shadcn/ui.

### Summary
Soryouth CRM transforms Solar EPC operations from a series of disjointed tasks into a streamlined, data-driven engine. By bridging the gap between sales, engineering, and maintenance, we empower renewable energy companies to scale faster and deliver superior customer experiences.
